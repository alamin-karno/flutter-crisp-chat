## About

**Md. Al-Amin** (also known as **Alamin Karno**) is a passionate software engineer and mobile application developer from **Dhaka, Bangladesh**. He is the creator and primary maintainer of the **Flutter Crisp Chat** plugin, which has been helping Flutter developers integrate Crisp live chat into their apps since 2022.

Al-Amin currently works at **[Vivasoft Limited](https://vivasoftltd.com/)**, a leading offshore software development company, where he focuses on building high-quality mobile applications using Flutter and native technologies.

## Open Source

Al-Amin is an active open source contributor. The Flutter Crisp Chat plugin is one of his key projects, providing the Flutter community with a reliable bridge to the Crisp customer messaging platform.

### Flutter Crisp Chat Stats

- **First published:** 2022
- **Current version:** 2.4.4
- **Platform:** pub.dev ([crisp_chat](https://pub.dev/packages/crisp_chat))
- **License:** MIT
- **Native SDKs:** Crisp Android SDK `2.0.16` · Crisp iOS SDK `~> 2.13.0`
